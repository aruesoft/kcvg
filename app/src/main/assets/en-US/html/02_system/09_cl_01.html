The cooling system maintains the proper engine temperature for maximum efficiency and performance by cooling down engine heat. It prevents the engine from being damaged by overheating.
Symptom
  1. Engine may become deformed due to overheating
  2. Fuel consumption increases (Poor performance due to improper temperature)
  3. Low engine power and noise
  4. Lubricant coating may become damaged
