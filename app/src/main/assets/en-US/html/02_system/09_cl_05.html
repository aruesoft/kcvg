Water Pump

The water pump circulates the coolant by rotating the impeller, which is operated by the drive belt.
Symptom
  1. Coolant leaks can be caused by damage to the water pump or gasket
  2. The engine may over heat because of damage to the impeller (Coolant cannot be circulated)
  3. A noise may be heard if there is damage to the bearing
