Sunroof

Panorama Sunroof General Sunroof
The sunroof allows sunlight and fresh air to enter the passenger compartment through the roof of the vehicle. When the sunroof switch is operated, a motor is controlled to open the sunshade and glass. It is designed to keep water and air out of the passenger compartment at the closed position.
Symptom
  1. Rainwater inflows
  2. Noise occurs when operating
