Overview

Main brand
Kia Motors launched the environmentally-friendly brand ECO Dynamics in 2009 as part of their firm commitment to the pursuit of zero emissions. In 2012, Kia developed a new eco brand system and designed a new emblem with a streamlined shape to convey the new futuristic and eco-friendly image. ECO Green, the unique color for the new emblem, was selected to complete the premium design and improve visibility.
Sub brand
Kia began their eco-movement starting with the Forte Hybrid introduced in 2009, followed by the Optima Hybrid & Ray EV in 2011, and more recently the Soul EV in 2014.
