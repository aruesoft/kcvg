Electric vehicles have an electric motor and high voltage battery instead of an internal combustion engine.
  1. High voltage battery

    Stores electric energy

  2. Traction motor

    Drives the vehicle using electric energy from the high voltage battery

  3. OBC

    Converts external AC power to DC power and charges the high voltage battery
