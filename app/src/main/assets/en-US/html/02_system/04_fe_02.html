Fuel Tank

The fuel tank is designed to store fuel safely. The fuel flows through the filler hose to the fuel tank. When starting the vehicle, the fuel stored in fuel tank is supplied by the fuel pump through the fuel line.
The fuel tank prevents fuel from causing accidental fires, in addition to storing the fuel.
Symptom
  1. Fuel leak
  2. Poor fuel supply
  3. Evaporation gas leak
