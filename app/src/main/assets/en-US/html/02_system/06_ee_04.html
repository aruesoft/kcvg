The starter is composed of a solenoid switch assembly, amateur assembly, pinion gear, etc.
When the ignition switch is turned on, the battery supplies electricity to the solenoid of the starter.
The lever first pushes the plunger, then the pinion gear engages the ring gear, thus rotating the flywheel. (Engine cranking)
After starting the engine, the overrunning clutch operates to idle the pinion gear so that the flywheel torque is not transmitted to the starter.
(to prevent damaging the starter)
Symptom
  1. Poor starting (no cranking or slow cranking)
  2. The starter keeps on spinning even after the vehicle starts
