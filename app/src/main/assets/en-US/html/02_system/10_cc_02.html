General AC compressor
Electric AC compressor
There are two types of AC compressors: a general AC compressor and an electric AC compressor.
The general AC compressor is used for gasoline/diesel vehicles. It is driven by the engine of a vehicle (connected by drive belt).
The electric AC compressor is used for hybrid/electric vehicles. It is driven by battery power.
The A/C compressor converts the torque obtained from an external source of power into a reciprocating up-and-down movement of the pistons.
In doing so, it compresses the refrigerant into high temperature & pressure refrigerant.
Symptom
  1. Poor cooling performance due to malfuction of the AC compressor
  2. An abnormal noise may occur if there is slip between AC pulley and drive belt (for general type AC compressor).
  3. A noise may be heard if there is damage to the bearing.
