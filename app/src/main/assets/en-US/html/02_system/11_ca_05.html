AVM (Around View Monitoring)

The AVM (Around View Monitoring) system allows video monitoring 360 degrees around the vehicle. This system consists of 4 cameras (front bumper, both side mirrors, rear bumper). Images from each camera are merged to provide a sky-view image of the vehicle’s surrounding area.
Symptom
If the sky-view image is not displayed correctly, AVM cameras should be calibrated as described in the service manual.
