    Charging Indicator

Electric vehicles (EV) or plug-in hybrid electric vehicles (PHEV) should be kept charged, similar to keeping a standard internal combustion vehicle fueled with gasoline.

There are two types of power to charge, alternating current (AC) and direct current (DC).

High voltage batteries use DC power, so in order to charge the vehicle using AC power, it must be converted to DC power. This is the role of the on-board charger (OBC). The OBC converts AC power into DC power and delivers it to the high voltage battery to recover the electric energy.

Symptom
  1. The high voltage battery may not be charged using AC power
